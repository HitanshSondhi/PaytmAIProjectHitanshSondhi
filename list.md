# Voice Commands - Voice Ledger

A comprehensive list of voice queries you can use with the Voice Ledger application.

---

## 📊 Collection & Analytics

| Command | What it does |
|---------|--------------|
| "aaj ka collection batao" | Get today's total collection with breakdown |
| "kal ke due payments" | Show list of pending payments due |

---

## 💳 Add Customer Credit (Udhaar)

| Command | What it does |
|---------|--------------|
| "[Customer Name] ke account main [amount] rupees add kardo" | Add credit to customer account |
| "[Customer Name] ke khata mein [amount] daal do" | Add credit (alternative phrase) |

**Examples:**
- "Madhur Kumar ke account main 2000 rupees add kardo"
- "Rahul ke khata mein 5000 daal do"

---

## 📈 Credit Score

| Command | What it does |
|---------|--------------|
| "[Customer Name] ka credit score batao" | Get customer's credit score & category |

**Examples:**
- "Rahul ka credit score batao"
- "Madhur ka credit score batao"

---

## 💰 Record Customer Payments

| Command | What it does |
|---------|--------------|
| "[Customer Name] ne [amount] rupees diye" | Record payment from customer |
| "[amount] rupees [Customer Name] se mila" | Record payment (alternative phrase) |

**Examples:**
- "Rahul ne 1000 rupees diye"
- "5000 rupees Madhur se mila"

---

## 📋 Customer Due Status

| Command | What it does |
|---------|--------------|
| "[Customer Name] ka due kya hai" | Check total amount due from customer |

**Examples:**
- "Rahul ka due kya hai"
- "Madhur ka due kya hai"

---

## ✅ Confirmation Commands

When prompted for confirmation (e.g., low credit score warning):

| Command | What it does |
|---------|--------------|
| "Yes" / "Haan" | Confirm the transaction |
| "No" / "Nahi" | Cancel the transaction |

---

## 🌐 Supported Languages

- **Hindi (hi-IN)** - Primary language
- **Tamil (ta-IN)**
- **Telugu (te-IN)**
- **English (en-IN)**

---

## 💡 Tips

1. Speak clearly and naturally in Hindi
2. Include customer name and amount clearly
3. Wait for the orb to indicate it's listening before speaking
4. For risky customers (low credit score), you'll be asked to confirm

---

## 🎯 Quick Demo Commands

```
1. "aaj ka collection batao"           → View today's collection

3. "rahul ke khata mein 5000 daal do"  → Add ₹5000 to Rahul
```
